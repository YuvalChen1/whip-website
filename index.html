Unleash Ultimate Cursor Control

More than just a visual gimmick. WHIP Cursor balances physics-based entertainment with high-efficiency productivity tools.

Realistic Physics Engine

Experience realistic rope physics. The whip string moves dynamically following your cursor's acceleration, weight, and friction, creating satisfying flicks at 60 FPS.

Practical Prompt Injection

Switch to Practical Mode to toggle off visual physics and turn your cursor into a prompt power tool. Instantly insert reusable prompt templates into any text inputs.

Dynamic Audio Triggers

Get instant acoustic satisfaction. Whip cracks are triggered by velocity thresholds. Each weapon has unique audio profiles, including flies buzzing and water splashing.

Interactive Customization

Fine-tune variables directly within the extension popup. Edit length, slot scale, grip size, and trace color to build a whip style that fits your display layout.
